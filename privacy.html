Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12/31/25

Kelley’s Candles respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you choose to share with us. This policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and your rights as a visitor or customer.

Information We Collect

We only collect personal information that you voluntarily provide through our website, primarily through Google Forms, such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Shipping or billing information (if placing an order)

Payment information is handled securely by third-party payment processors and is not stored by Kelley’s Candles.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only to:

  • Respond to inquiries or requests
  • Process and fulfill orders
  • Send optional email updates or promotions (you may unsubscribe at any time)

We do not use your information for automated decision-making or profiling.

Google Forms & Third-Party Services

We use Google Forms to collect and manage form submissions. Information submitted through these forms is stored securely within Google’s systems and is subject to Google’s privacy practices. Kelley’s Candles only accesses this information for business purposes related to your request.

Sharing Your Information

Kelley’s Candles does not sell or rent your personal information. We may share limited information with trusted third-party services (such as payment processors or shipping providers) only when necessary to complete an order or comply with legal requirements.

Cookies & Site Data

Our website may use basic cookies or analytics to ensure proper functionality and improve user experience. You may disable cookies in your browser, though some site features may be limited.

Your Rights

You may:

  • Opt out of marketing emails at any time
  • Request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below.

Children’s Privacy

Kelley’s Candles does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information, but no method of online transmission or storage is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
