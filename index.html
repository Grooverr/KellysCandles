About Us

Kelley's Farm Candles started the way most good things do. Quietly, on a kitchen counter, with a pour pot and a lot of trial and error.

We're a small family operation, and every candle is still made by hand the same way it was from the very beginning: natural soy wax, carefully chosen fragrance oils, and cotton wicks set in thick glass jars. No shortcuts, no assembly lines. Just small batches poured when the time is right.

The scents we carry are ones we'd actually want burning in our own home. Apple Pie on a cool fall morning, Lavender winding down a long day, Black Raspberry Vanilla Bean just because it smells incredible. We keep the lineup tight and rotate seasonally, so if something catches your eye, it's worth grabbing while it's in stock.

We offer local pickup and nationwide shipping. Thank you for supporting something small and handmade. It genuinely means a lot to us.

Frequently Asked Questions

Shipping

How long does shipping take?

Orders are typically processed within 3-5 business days, then shipped via USPS. Transit time is usually 2-5 additional days depending on your location. You'll receive a tracking number by email once your order ships.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! Orders over $100 ship free. Shipping rates for smaller orders are calculated at checkout based on your location.

Do you offer local pickup?

Yes, local pickup is available. Select that option at checkout and we'll coordinate a time with you by email.

Products

What are your candles made of?

All of our candles are hand-poured in small batches using natural soy wax, cotton wicks, and fragrance oils. We don't use paraffin or synthetic additives.

How long do the candles burn?

Burn times vary by size: the 17oz 3-wick burns approximately 50-60 hours, the 12oz mason jar around 40-50 hours, and the 6oz jar around 20-25 hours. Trimming your wick before each burn helps maximize burn time.

Are scents always available?

We craft in small batches and scent availability can vary by season. The inventory on this page updates automatically so what you see is what we currently have in stock.

What are wax melts and how do I use them?

Wax melts are wickless scented wax designed for use with an electric or tea-light wax warmer. Simply place one or two cubes in your warmer and enjoy the scent without an open flame. They're a great option for spaces where candles aren't practical.

Orders & Returns

What is your return policy?

Due to the handmade nature of our products, all sales are generally final. If your candle arrives damaged or there's an issue with your order, please email us right away and we'll make it right.

What if my candle arrives broken?

We pack carefully, but occasionally things happen in transit. Send us a photo of the damage to our email within 7 days of delivery and we'll send a replacement or issue a refund.

Can I change or cancel my order?

We start preparing orders quickly, but reach out by email as soon as possible and we'll do our best to accommodate changes before your order ships.
