About Us

Kelley's Farm Candles started the way most good things do. Quietly, on a kitchen counter, with a pour pot and a lot of trial and error.

We're a small family operation, and every candle is still made by hand the same way it was from the very beginning: natural soy wax, carefully chosen fragrance oils, and cotton wicks set in thick glass jars. No shortcuts, no assembly lines. Just small batches poured when the time is right.

The scents we carry are ones we'd actually want burning in our own home. Apple Pie on a cool fall morning, Lavender winding down a long day, Black Raspberry Vanilla Bean just because it smells incredible. We keep the lineup tight and rotate seasonally, so if something catches your eye it's worth grabbing while it's in stock.

We offer local pickup and nationwide shipping. Thank you for supporting something small and handmade. It genuinely means a lot to us.

Frequently Asked Questions

Shipping

How long does shipping take?

Orders are typically processed and poured within 3–5 business days, then shipped via USPS. Transit time is usually 2–5 additional days depending on your location. You'll receive a tracking number by email once your order ships.

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! Orders over $100 ship free. Orders under $100 are charged a flat shipping rate calculated at checkout based on your location and order weight.

Can I pick up locally?

Absolutely. Local pickup is available — just select that option at checkout and we'll reach out to coordinate a time.

Candle Care & Ingredients

What are your candles made of?

We use 100% natural soy wax, premium fragrance oils, and cotton wicks. Our candles are hand-poured in small batches right here on the farm.

How do I get the best burn from my candle?

On the very first burn, let the wax melt all the way to the edges of the jar — this prevents tunneling and ensures you get the full life out of your candle. Trim the wick to 1/4 inch before each burn, and don't burn for more than four hours at a time. For the full guide, see our Candle Care & Safety page.

Are your candles safe around pets?

Our candles use soy wax and fragrance oils that are safe for general home use. That said, we always recommend keeping any burning candle out of reach of pets and never leaving one unattended. If your pet has known sensitivities to scented products, consult your vet.

Orders & Returns

Can I return or exchange a candle?

Because our candles are handmade food-adjacent products, we aren't able to accept returns on opened items. If your candle arrives damaged or there's an issue with your order, please email us right away and we'll make it right.

What if my candle arrives broken?

We pack carefully, but occasionally things happen in transit. Send us a photo of the damage to our email within 7 days of delivery and we'll send a replacement or issue a refund.

Can I change or cancel my order?

We start preparing orders quickly, but reach out by email as soon as possible and we'll do our best to accommodate changes before your order ships.
